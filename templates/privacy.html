Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any notes you provide. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as directed by us. We do not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.
